WASD - move forward, left, backward, right
Arrow keys - pitch, yaw camera
IJKLOP - light movement
1,2 - toggle front face culling
3,4 - toggle wireframe mode
5,6,7 - toggle point, directional, spotlight

DEBUG
Z to turn on spatial partition rendering
F,G,H,B to override all LODs
F - Low res
G - Med res
H - High res
B - Change back to default

LCLick to shoot
RClick to throw grenade
R to reload

Gameplay:
Defend the base by killing enemies
There is a statue thing you can activate by shooting the button on the right to it
